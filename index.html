Personal Site

A simple yet elegant two-column page with links to all of my work and personal projects. The HTML is semantic and I use one media query to make the page responsive. There is a little bit of JavaScript for a silly excuse generator. Because my design is heavily typographic, I used glyphhanger to subset my fonts in order to improve the performance. It scores well on PageSpeed Insights, Yellow Lab Test, and DebugBear.

Drag-N-Draw

A pixelart sketchpad made with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS. The pixels are colored in by hovering rather than clicking because I wanted to simulate the effect of an Etch-A-Sketch; that is, you have to be strategic about how you enter and exit the board. Some features I would like to add in the future: a color picker, the ability to save and download your work, and mobile functionality.

Dupe Checker

A webapp that checks for duplicated words and phrases in a body of text. It is intended for writers who like to sweat the details. You can toggle the minumum number of letters that count (to stop words such as "the" from rendering your list useless). You can then edit your text in the browser and recheck it. I am building the kind of app that I want to use when I am writing. It is still in progress, but I have made the demo live in the spirit of "working with the garage door up."
